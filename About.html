About ABC Fitness Studio

ABC Fitness Studio is a local fitness haven specializing in a diverse range of classes, including yoga, Pilates, and Zumba—all geared toward promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. With a strong commitment to well-being, we aim to provide our community with a welcoming space for both physical and mental wellness.

In addition to our classes, we offer a curated selection of high-quality fitness and training equipment to support your fitness journey. While we’ve previously operated without an online presence, this website is our next step to reflect our core values of balance, harmony, and active living.

Our primary goal is to increase visibility, foster stronger community connections, and make it easy to inquire about classes, make bookings, or purchase equipment directly.

  • Saturday: 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
